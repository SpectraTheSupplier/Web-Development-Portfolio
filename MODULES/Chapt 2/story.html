Toy Story 2

Overview

Toy Story 2 is a 1999 animated film produced by Pixar Animation Studios and released by Walt Disney Pictures. The story follows Woody, who is stolen by a toy collector, prompting Buzz Lightyear and the rest of the gang to embark on a mission to rescue him.

Cast

  • Tom Hanks as Woody
  • Tim Allen as Buzz Lightyear
  • Joan Cusack as Jessie
  • Kelsey Grammer as Stinky Pete

Characters

Some of the most beloved characters include Woody, Buzz Lightyear, Jessie, Stinky Pete, and the lovable Slinky Dog.
